Trekking Destinations Uttarakhand. The many thrills Uttarakhand has no offer from treks to while water rafting to paragliding are a lure for any traveler wanting an adrenaline kick, Go on discover the highs of living. Uttarakhand offers the most challenging hills for seasoned trekkers & the most scenic trails for beginners

Most trails in the hilly terrain are without litter and crowds. The villagers you encounter en route are extremely hospitable without expecting bakshish for any help that may offer you.Even the wildlife here is more curious then frightened! But do note that for independent, serious rekkers, often finding guides is not simple, and for beginners, walking uphill may just prove a bit tougher that it is on the plans.
